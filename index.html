CV | Google Scholar | ORCID | LinkedIn | GitHub

I am a physician at Taichung Veterans General Hospital with a Ph.D. from China Medical University under the supervision of Academician Wen-Hwa Lee at the Genomics Research Center, Academia Sinica. My doctoral research focused on metabolic mechanisms of cancer initiation, specifically investigating how high-sugar diets induce pancreatic DNA damage through O-GlcNAcylation and nucleotide metabolism disruption.

I am skilled in mining large-scale hospital datasets (400K+ samples) with SQL and bioinformatics pipelines. My experience spans genome-wide association studies (GWAS), case-control study design, LC-MS metabolomics, and high-content imaging assays applicable to drug screening workflows. I combine proficiency in R, Python, and Linux with deep biomedical knowledge.

I am actively seeking to apply my scientific and analytical acumen to biotech investment analysis and drug development evaluation. My research and clinical experience provide unique insights into translating scientific discoveries into therapeutic opportunities.
